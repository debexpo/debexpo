Welcome to debexpo

Debexpo is a web app where::

  • Package maintainers can upload source packages, so that they can be reviewed by "Sponsors"
  • Sponsors (Debian developers) can review packages and provide feedback.

Once you upload packages, you should usually send an email to the Debian mentors mailing list indicating that you want someone to review your work. See also Section 2.2 of the New Maintainer's Guide.

You can read more about debexpo on the Debian Wiki.

You can also get directly with the debexpo team. Asheesh Laroia and Andrey Rahmatullin are the main people involved. For now, the best way to reach them is by emailing the debexpo development list. You can find information about that on the Debian wiki page above.

debexpo has had a long history. Andrey (wRAR) and Asheesh (paulproteus) are bringing it back, and we need help. We hope you'll hang out in #debexpo on irc.debian.org and chat with us.
